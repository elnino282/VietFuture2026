## Design Context

### Users
The platform serves a dual-context user base:
- **Farmers**: Primarily use the app on mobile devices outdoors, often under bright harsh sunlight (glare), possibly with dirty or gloved hands. Their primary job is managing crops on the field.
- **Buyers, Employees, and Admins**: Primarily use desktop interfaces indoors in office environments for data analysis, documentation, and verifying traceability.

### Brand Personality
- **Personality**: Trustworthy, Organic, Crisp.
- **Emotions**: The interface should evoke *Trust* (crucial for VietGAP compliance and sales), *Approachability* (to not intimidate older farmers), and *Technical Precision* (for buyers verifying data).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Interfaces that blend natural elements with precise utility (e.g., modern consumer health apps, premium grocery e-commerce, or tools like Linear for the Admin side but warmer).
- **Theme**: Both Light and Dark modes are used. The platform employs distinct portals with tailored semantic color tokens, and deliberate organic asymmetry to component styling (e.g., cards).
- **Anti-references**: Explicitly MUST NOT look like a complex, sterile corporate ERP (e.g., SAP/Oracle), a generic Web3/Crypto dashboard, or a "toy" app with overly cartoonish farm graphics.

### Design Principles
1. **Adaptive & High-Contrast**: Maintain extreme high-contrast modes for outdoor visibility (avoiding subtle light-gray on white text) to combat screen glare in the field.
2. **Accessible Interaction**: Ensure minimum 44x44px touch targets across the Farmer portal to accommodate use while moving or with gloved hands.
3. **Low Cognitive Load**: Design for an older demographic by avoiding jargon, always pairing clear iconography with text (never icons alone), and offering a highly guided AI assistant experience to reduce the learning curve.
4. **Organic Precision**: Balance approachability with professionalism. Avoid corporate sterility while ensuring the interface communicates the technical precision required for trusted traceability data.
